Players take the role of space‚Äëshepherds guiding various animal species into their cages. Each species has unique behaviors and score values. A UFO roams above the game arena, occasionally spawning new animals. The goal is to gather animals efficiently within the time limit‚Äîhighest score wins.

## **Phase 1 ‚Äî Core Gameplay Foundation**
### **1. Player Controls & Movement**
- Smooth WASD/joystick movement.

### **2. Animal Basic Behaviors**

- Persistent list stored per player.
- Unlock entry only when a species is inside cage *at match end* **and** the team wins.
- UI for viewing discovered species.
